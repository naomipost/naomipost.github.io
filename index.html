Reflecting on My First Semester

Introduction

Most of the time, I don't consider myself a creative person. For that reason, I had a hard time deciding what I wanted to do for the synthesis project. I sat and thought about it for a while, and then remembered a video that had popped up in my YouTube recommended a few weeks ago: Learn HTML in 1 Hour. I watched the video and made a pointless silly "website," which was really just a few HTML files that linked together. I had a lot of fun doing that, but I had no real reason to make a website, so I stopped messing around after a while and forgot about it until I was thinking of what project I wanted to make. I decided it would be fun to challenge myself to build a real website from scratch. I also figured that considering that my major is computer science, it's probably a good idea to get some experience creating websites.
